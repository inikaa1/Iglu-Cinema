About Us

The Movie Pod is a private cinematic experience designed for YOU!

We aim to create a place for all youngsters to be able to enjoy their movie watching experience ranging from first class service to quality projection. Inspired by the principles of the Iglu student accommoadation, we guarantee your time spent here will be full of laughter and excitement.

6. How can I cancel or change the timing of my booking?

You can go on your account information where you can see your previous bookings. After clicking on it, there will be an option to either cancel or change the booking timing

8. What are the payment methods accepted by the system?

Visa, Mastercard and EFTPOS are accepted at the reception.
Note: EFTPOS has a 35c surcharge. Visa and Mastercard have a 1.75% surcharge.
