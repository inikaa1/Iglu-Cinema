Display Options

We offer two display options for our users.

We have a wide range of movies that we offer. You can visit the movie selection page and check if your movie is available. If your movie is available we will be able to project it for you in the Cinema. You just need to select "Movie from Selection" when you are making a booking. After your booking has been confirmed, simply come during your booking timming and let the reception know which movie you would like to play.

Additionally If your movie is not in the movie selection, please feel free to go to the our services tab and fill the feedback form to add the movie selection.


We also offer the option to play a movie from your own device. Our system is compatible with all apple devices and allows airplay to an apple TV. Additionally, you can also temporaryly add login to third party applications much as netflix, disney+ or amazon prime. You can connect your phone, ipad, or laptop manully through the following cables: lightning, micro USB, HDMI. We do not provide device extentions, and request you to bring your own extention if the ports mentioned are not available on your device.
